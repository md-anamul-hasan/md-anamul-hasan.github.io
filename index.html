  • Automobile Engine Oil Bottle Design

    4 Variant – 1L, 2L, 4L & 5L

    Project Overview:
    This personal project involves the design of four distinct variants of automobile engine oil bottles, with capacities of 1L, 2L, 4L, and 5L respectively. The central focus of this design endeavor is to optimize production costs through the utilization of injection blow molding techniques, while also ensuring optimal functionality.

    Component Manufacturing Process Material
    Cap Injection Molding PP
    Body Blow Molding HDPE

    The primary objective of this project is to design an assortment of automobile engine oil bottles that seamlessly integrate cost-effective production methods, specifically focusing on injection blow molding. Additionally, these bottles are crafted to effectively fulfill their intended purpose while adhering to the highest quality standards.
